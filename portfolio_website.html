Hi, my name is Ryan Bennett.

I'm a designer from Northern Ireland. I'm currently studying Interaction design at the Belfast School of Art. I’m interested in other mediums such as drawing and painting, but I’m looking at perfecting my skills in more graphic forms of design, such as UX/UI design and illustration.

My Mission.

I want to create memorable designs that leave a lasting impression on people. I want to bring your design to life.

My values are...

Personality.

I want all my projects to tell a story from start to finish.

Quality.

I want my work to be the best it can be. I listen to advice and use it to improve my work

Hard-working.

I want to give a 100% to all my projects, so that I achieve the best outcome. I am a bit of a perfectionist so I won't be happy until I get it right.
